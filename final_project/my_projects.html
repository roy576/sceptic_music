SCEPT!C is an experimental music project by Roy that blends ambient, synthesizer-driven soundscapes with hypnotic rhythms and evolving textures. Drawing inspiration from Indian musical influences and artists like Øneheart, Clams Casino, Alaskan Tapes, and Aphex Twin, SCEPT!C merges ambient, wave, and experimental sounds to create immersive, cinematic experiences. The music combines deep synth pads, atmospheric layers, and organic tones, transporting listeners to other worlds. Each track evokes deep emotions, offering a mix of calm, introspection, and complex sonic landscapes. From soothing, ethereal sounds to mesmerizing beats, the music invites exploration of inner space. Perfect for relaxation, focus, or creative work, SCEPT!C provides a rich backdrop for meditation or deep listening. It offers a transformative experience, helping listeners disconnect from the chaos of everyday life. Whether for peace or sonic adventure, SCEPT!C provides an unforgettable journey through sound.

SCEPT!C’s sound also includes gritty, underground hip-hop, drawing heavy inspiration from producers like Wondagurl, Kenny Beats, Decap, and JPEGMAFIA. The beats are raw and energetic, built with swing-heavy rhythms that make your head nod. SCEPT!C blends ethnic samples with innovative chops, creating unique, textured sounds that are both hard-hitting and smooth. The focus is on exceptional sampling, with tracks that incorporate bold, unconventional elements alongside the raw vibe of underground hip-hop. It’s a fusion of head-bopping rhythms, sharp percussion, and layered sounds designed to keep you engaged, vibing, and thinking. Expect hip-hop beats that push the boundaries of traditional sound, with a style that’s as dynamic as it is distinct.
